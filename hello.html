What's included in the Program

App marketing employs strategies to enhance mobile app visibility, downloads, and usage through a mix of traditional and digital methods, primarily targeting platforms such as Apple's App Store and Google Play Store.

  • App Store Optimization (ASO)
  • User Acquisition Strategies
  • Retention and Engagement Tactics
  • App Monetization Models
  • Analytics and Optimization
  • App Updates and Feedback Management

This course caters to digital marketers, app developers, product managers, business owners, and anyone pursuing a career in app marketing.
